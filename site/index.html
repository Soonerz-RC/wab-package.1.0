Key investment highlights
01

Held-by-Production foundation

Nine HBP ORRI sections plus thirty-six owned tracts with active producing leases beneath them. Confirmed production from premier Anadarko Basin operators including Devon Energy — whose Mallard development on OR005 (Custer 26-14N-20W) currently runs six wells across the section. The HBP base anchors the package against commodity downside: cash flow that does not depend on new drilling.

02

Western Anadarko Basin concentration

Eighty-seven tracts across forty-three unique sections, focused exclusively in four counties of the Western Anadarko Basin — Caddo, Custer, Roger Mills, and Washita. Premier exposure to the Tonkawa, Cleveland, Granite Wash, Red Fork, Cherokee, and Atoka formations. The WAB remains among the most economic U.S. unconventional regions, with sustained operator activity and infrastructure buildout.

03

Active near-term development catalysts

Five active OCC drilling permits and seventy-nine regulatory actions touch owned tracts. Upland Operating is leading the leasing campaign in 12N-23W and 13N-23W, with 82 recent leases recorded directly on owned sections — a position directly adjacent to the non-HBP ORRIs in the package. Mewbourne Oil Company, Devon Energy, Reign Operating, and Crawley Petroleum are also actively developing across the WAB. The backdrop translates directly into pooling, spacing, and drilling momentum on the legals where assets are owned.

04

Curated inventory, experienced sponsor

Hand-selected over multiple acquisition cycles through GBK International Group, Ltd and TPC Minerals, LLC. The package mixes mineral fee interests and overriding royalty interests across HBP and non-HBP positions — diversification within the package itself, both for current cash flow and for future drilling upside.
